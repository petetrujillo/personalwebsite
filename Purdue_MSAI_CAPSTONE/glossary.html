Parent Glossary

Here are plain-language definitions for terms you'll see on the dashboard. [cite: 1397]

  • Conceptual Gap

    A key insight that identifies when a student knows "what" to answer but not "why." It flags cases where a student gives a factually correct answer (high Correctness score) but provides a weak or confusing explanation (low Explanation Quality score). [cite: 964-966]

  • Explanation Quality Score

    This evaluates how well the student explains their reasoning. It looks for clarity, logical reasoning, and original thought, not just memorized facts. [cite: 1154-1155]

  • Correctness Score

    This evaluates if a student's answer is factually correct when compared to the approved lesson materials. [cite: 1143]

  • AI Bias

    If the data used to teach an AI is unfair, incomplete, or reflects human biases, the AI will also produce unfair results. [cite: 2007]

  • AI Hallucination

    Sometimes, AI models can confidently state things that are completely wrong or made up. [cite: 2009] This is why all answers are based on approved school content only.
